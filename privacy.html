Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Ascora Studio ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect through this website and how we use it.

What we collect

When you submit a project inquiry, we collect the information you provide: your name, company name, email address, phone number, industry, project type, budget range, goal, and any project details. We also receive standard technical data (such as your approximate region and the pages you visit) for security and analytics.

How we use it

  • To respond to your inquiry and discuss your project.
  • To prepare proposals and manage our relationship with you.
  • To improve our website and understand how it is used.

We do not sell your data, and we never use it for purposes you wouldn't reasonably expect.

Where it's stored

Inquiries are stored securely in our customer database (hosted on Supabase, EU region) with row-level security. Access is restricted to authorized studio members only.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, and you may withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, email hello@ascorastudio.com.

Retention

We keep inquiry data only as long as needed to serve you or as required by law, after which it is deleted.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hello@ascorastudio.com.
